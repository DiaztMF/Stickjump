About Stick Jump

Stick Jump is a thrilling 2D platformer inspired by classics, combining nostalgic gameplay with fresh challenges. Control our agile hero as he leaps across treacherous gaps, avoids cunning traps, and outsmarts various foes to reach the finish line.
